What is Hayatuk?

Hayatuk (“your life”) is a free mobile app that brings blood donors and people in need together in their local community. When someone needs blood, nearby donors with the right blood type are notified — so help can arrive when it matters most.

For donors

See active blood requests around you on a live map and respond to the ones you can help with.

For those in need

Post a request when you or a loved one needs blood, and reach willing donors nearby.

Matched by blood type

Notifications go to compatible donors close by, so the right people are reached fast.

How it works

From request to donation in a few simple steps.

1

Discover nearby requests

Open the map to see blood requests around you, or get notified when your blood type is needed.

2

Accept a request

Choose a request you can help with. You and the requester then get each other’s contact to coordinate.

3

Donate

Meet at the agreed location and donate. Mark it done in the app when finished.

4

Or ask for help

Need blood? Post a request and connect with willing donors near you.

Why we built Hayatuk

Blood can’t be manufactured — it can only come from donors. Yet too often, patients and families are left searching their personal networks at the worst possible moment, hoping to find a compatible donor in time. We believe no one should have to wait for blood when willing donors are nearby.

Hayatuk turns local goodwill into timely action: a simple, trustworthy way for communities to give blood to the people around them. Every donor who joins makes the network stronger — and brings someone’s loved one closer to the help they need.

Privacy by design

Helping others should never cost you your privacy. Hayatuk is built to share as little as possible.

  • Your exact location never leaves your device — only an approximate area is used to find nearby requests.
  • Your phone number is shared only after you accept a request, so you can coordinate the donation.
  • You control your availability — turn donor mode on or off anytime.
  • You can delete your account and your data at any time, right from the app.
  • No ads and no third-party advertising trackers.
